Fun facts and stuff

  • Originally written by Taylor while she was writing for Red (Stolen Version), "Babe" was eventually given to Sugarland, and then released as a vault track on Red (Taylor's Version).
  • The Sugarland version is slightly different than the version Taylor recorded for Red TV, and you can also hear Taylor in the background on the original version.
