Fun facts and stuff

  • When Red was originally released, Taylor mentioned that she had whittled the original version of All Too Well down to 10 minutes from 25 minutes, and she had her co-writer help her get it down to only 5 minutes. Since then, fans have been begging for her to release the original or 10 minute version.
  • The 10-Minute version of ATW comes with a short film.
  • It also debuted at #1 on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • The 10-Minute version of ATW broke the record previously held by Don Mclean's "American Pie (Part I & II)" as the longest song to top the charts on the Billboard Hot 100 charts.
