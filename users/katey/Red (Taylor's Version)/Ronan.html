Fun facts and stuff

  • Taylor wrote "Ronan" based on a blog she followed about a boy with terminal childhood cancer. She gave Maya, Ronan's mother, a co-writing credit on the song.
  • "Ronan" was originally released for Stand Up To Cancer, but Taylor wanted to include it on Red TV, and Maya (Ronan's mom) was incredibly supportive.
  • "Ronan" is on my playlist of songs that make me instantly cry.
