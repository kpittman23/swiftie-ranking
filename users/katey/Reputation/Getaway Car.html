Fun facts and stuff

  • While dating Calvin Harris, Taylor met boyfriend Tom Hiddleston, and eventual boyfriend Joe Alwyn at the 2016 MET gala. "Getaway Car" is about her relationship(s) with these 3.
  • Despite dating Calvin Harris for over a year, "Getaway Car" is one of the only songs that even references him, as he is the one she was "getting away" from when she starting seeing Tom Hiddleston.
