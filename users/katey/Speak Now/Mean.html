Fun facts and stuff

  • It is rumored that Taylor Swift wrote "Mean" in response to scathing reviews by blogger Bob Lefsetz, who wrote poorly of Swift about her performance with Stevie Nicks at the 2010 Grammy Awards
  • Ironically, "Mean" won Best Country Solo Performance and Best Country Song at the 2012 Grammy Awards.
