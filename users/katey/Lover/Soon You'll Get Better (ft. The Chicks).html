Fun facts and stuff

  • The Chicks provide vocal backup on this song.
  • Taylor wrote this song for her mother, Andrea Swift, who has been struggling with cancer since 2015.
  • Taylor performed this song as a part of the Global Citizens One World Together at Home performance, where artists performed from home during the Coronavirus pandemic
