Fun facts and stuff

  • "betty" is part of the "Teenage Love Triangle" on folklore, along with "cardigan" and "august".
  • The names in the song, Betty, James, and Inez, are the names of Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds' children.
  • Taylor's boyfriend, Joe Alwyn, is credited as a songwriter because he came up with the chorus, which actually inspired Taylor to create the rest of the song.
