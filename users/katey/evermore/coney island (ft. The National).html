Fun facts and stuff

  • The bridge alludes to many other songs in Taylor's catalogue, but the final line, "when I walked up to the podium, I think that I forgot to say your name" refers to when she won AOTY for 1989, and didn't mention her then boyfriend Calvin Harris.
  • The National is a band from Cincinnati.
