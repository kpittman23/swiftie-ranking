Fun facts and stuff

  • "Shake It Off" was the first single released for 1989, spending 4 weeks at number one on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • In September 2017, songwriters Sean "Sep" Hall and Nate Butler sued Swift for copyright infringement. They alleged that the lyrics of "Shake It Off" plagiarized those of "Playas Gon' Play", and this lawsuit is still ongoing today after being dismissed multiple times.
