Fun facts and stuff

  • "Bad Blood" was written during a feud, now resolved, with Katy Perry, after Katy Perry stole Taylor's backup dancers.
  • The single version features Kendrick Lamar.
  • The video broke Vevo's 24-hour viewing record by accumulating 20.1 million views in its first day of release
  • "Bad Blood" also peaked at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 making it Taylor's third number one from 1989, and her fourth number one overall.
  • It also won a Grammy for Best Music Video.
