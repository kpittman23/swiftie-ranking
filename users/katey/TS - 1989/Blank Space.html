Fun facts and stuff

  • "Blank Space" peaked at number one on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • Taylor wrote "Blank Space" about the way the media painted her as a crazed and obsessed girlfriend.
  • Taylor included a voice memo of her coming up with the idea for this song on the deluxe version of 1989.
